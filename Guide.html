⬅ Return to Vault

Welcome to the Master Guardian System. This vault uses AES-256 encryption to keep your passwords private. Your data never leaves this device unless you choose to back it up.

1. Setting Your PIN

01 When you first open the vault, choose a 4-6 digit PIN. This PIN is your encryption key. If you forget it, the data cannot be unlocked without a recovery file.

2. Adding Passwords

Click the "+ New" button to open the entry form. You can store:

  • Site/Service: (e.g., Gmail, Netflix)
  • Username: Your login ID
  • Password: Use the 🎲 icon to generate a strong, random password.
  • Notes: Add security questions or account details here.
⚠️ Critical Step: Your Emergency Recovery Envelope is created/updated only when you save a password. Make sure to save at least one entry to stay protected!

3. Offline & App Installation

This vault is a PWA (Progressive Web App). You can install it on your device:

  • Desktop: Click the "Install" icon in the browser address bar.
  • Mobile: Tap "Add to Home Screen" in your browser menu.

Once installed, the vault works 100% offline without an internet connection.

4. Emergency Recovery

If you forget your PIN:

  1. Go to the Login Screen.
  2. Click "Get Support Recovery File (.vault)".
  3. Send that file to your Administrator (Beast).
  4. They will decrypt the file using the Master Key and provide you with your passwords.
